Financial & Accounting Services

Precise and efficient financial management is the foundation of stable business operations. Our team provides both operational and advisory support in all aspects of financial and accounting management — from day-to-day organization of documentation to strategic analysis and planning. In close cooperation with accounting services, we help ensure transparency, compliance, and timeliness of all financial obligations. Our services are flexible and tailored to the specific needs of each client.

What our financial & accounting services include:

  1. Preparation and organization of financial documentation: collecting and systematizing incoming and outgoing invoices; preparing documentation for accounting services; organizing archiving and storage of documents.
  2. Accounting support: recording payments and monitoring cash flows; creating and managing expense and revenue records; cooperating with the accountant and preparing documents for payroll, VAT, and other obligations; bookkeeping services.
  3. Management of liabilities and receivables: monitoring due dates and payment reminders; managing receivables collection; maintaining records of outstanding obligations and taking necessary steps.
  4. Financial advisory: analyzing the company’s financial situation; proposals for cost optimization and profit increase; assistance in budgeting and resource management.
  5. Tax support: providing information on tax obligations and deadlines; preparing documentation for tax audits; cooperating with tax advisors to ensure compliance.
  6. Preparation of financial reports: creating basic management reports (income and expense analysis); supporting the development of business plans and financial projections; collaborating on statutory financial statements.
  7. Investment tracking: maintaining records of assets and investments; assisting in analyzing return on investment (ROI).
  8. Personalized services: tailoring services to the specific needs of clients; flexible support for ad hoc projects or long-term obligations.

Note: Accounting services are carried out by an external certified accounting firm with our business coordination and support. Tax-related support is provided in cooperation with external tax experts. This way, clients are ensured a reliable and high-quality service without additional arrangements — everything can be coordinated in one place through our team.
