Wedding & Celebration Planning

Your most beautiful day – without the stress and worry over details. A wedding is a day remembered for a lifetime, and that's exactly why it shouldn't be the day the couple and family run around coordinating suppliers and checking whether everything is in place. We take over the organization and logistics of your wedding. You live the day just as you imagined it – surrounded by your loved ones, calm and fully present.

Why Providentia

Behind every wedding stands experience in organizing high-level events – from conferences and congresses to concerts and special occasions. That experience brings what matters most on your wedding day: peace of mind. The peace that comes from knowing someone reliably holds the reins while you enjoy.

One Point of Contact

You don't coordinate the florist, catering, music and venue yourself – it all goes through us.

A Plan That Holds

The timeline, budget and script arranged in advance, with no improvising on the day.

Your Style, Not a Template

We listen to how you envision your day and build it together.

As Much as You Need

What We Organize

The focus is on weddings – from intimate celebrations to large weddings with several hundred guests. Alongside weddings, we also organize christenings, anniversaries, jubilees, birthdays and other special occasions. Any celebration, any size – the approach is the same: a clear plan and warm, caring execution.

How It Works – From Idea to Memory

1. Conversation and Concept

We start with you: what kind of wedding you envision, what atmosphere you want, what your budget is. From that we build the concept, script and timeline for the whole day.

2. Venue and Atmosphere

We find and book a venue that matches your vision and organize the decor, sound and lighting so everything looks and feels exactly as you wanted.

3. Suppliers

We negotiate and coordinate catering, decorations, floral arrangements, music, the photographer and everything else. You don't request quotes or align deadlines – we take that on.

4. Decoration and Design

We design and set up the decoration, arrange the space and tables, and match every detail to the theme and mood of your celebration.

5. Invitations and Guests

If you wish, we create invitations, manage the guest list and track confirmed attendance so seating, catering and the seating plan are arranged in time.

6. On-the-Day Coordination

We run the schedule and protocol, coordinate suppliers and performers, and discreetly oversee execution. If something goes wrong, we fix it before anyone notices.

You can take all of the above as a whole or choose only the parts you need – depending on where your support is needed most.
