FAQ 

​How does business consulting work?

Business consulting begins with an analysis of your operations to identify opportunities for growth and improvement. We then define goals and develop tailored strategies that include process optimization, resolving operational issues and documentation, as well as increasing revenue or reducing costs.

Our consulting includes support in implementing solutions, monitoring results, and adjusting strategies as needed. Our goal is to ensure sustainable growth and success for your business through flexible consultations tailored to your specific challenges.

​What types of events do you organize?

We organize a wide range of business events, including:
→ conferences
→ seminars
→ teambuilding activities
→ corporate celebrations

Each event is tailored to the client's goals and needs.

​Do you offer virtual assistant services?

Yes, our virtual assistants are available with flexible schedules and can handle a variety of tasks, from administration to communication, in packages starting from just five hours per month.

​How do marketing and PR services work?

Marketing and PR are focused on building brand recognition and trust. Marketing promotes your products or services through strategies such as digital campaigns, advertising, and social media, while PR builds public relations through media communication, event organization, and reputation management.

By combining market analysis, creative campaigns, and clear messaging, we create strategies that reach your target audience, increase visibility, and strengthen your reputation.

​How do financial and accounting services work?

We provide administrative, financial, and accounting services in one place, including record keeping and preparation of all business documentation for submission and processing by a certified external accounting firm — our trusted professional partner. This means we handle the entire cooperation with the authorized external accounting service on your behalf. The preparation of business documentation includes everything from payroll-related documents to tax obligations and financial reports. By continuously monitoring your business performance and optimizing costs, we help you maintain financial stability and operational security. 

Note: Accounting services are provided by an external certified bookkeeping firm, with our business coordination and support. Tax-related support is delivered in collaboration with experienced external tax advisors. This way, we ensure our clients receive reliable and high-quality service without the need for additional arrangements — everything can be managed in one place, through our team.
